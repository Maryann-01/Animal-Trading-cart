German Shepherd

The German Shepherd or Alsatian is a German breed of working dog of medium to large size.It was originally bred as a herding dog, for herding sheep.

  • Scientific Name:Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length:60 to 65cm
  • Average Lifespan:9 to 13years
  • Habitat:In a House
